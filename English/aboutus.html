Who are we?

Rina Tokhi, Mahdia Naiebi, Esmatullah Niazi and Ahmad Edris Ludin . A group of four students from Kabul University faculty of computer science. You are most welcome to share your idea for betterment of the website and if you find any bug or issue in calculation of numbers please do not hesitate to inform us.
